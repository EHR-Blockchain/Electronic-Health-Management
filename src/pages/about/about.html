About EHR Management

WHAT IS EHR?

EHR stand for Electronic Health Record

It is basically nothing but your complete health history stored at one place instead of it being distributed across different databases ( in this case across different Hospital Databases

HOW TO USE THE APP?

This app is a platform agnostic app made on Ionic Framework
Using it is very simple. The salient features of the app are:-

1. The app can be used to access all your previous prescriptions with details such as the disease daignosed and the medicines prescribed.

2. The app can also be used to give a particular doctor access to your own health records by scanning the QR code present with the doctor.
